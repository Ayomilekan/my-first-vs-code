WOMEN DEVELOPMENT CENTER

We have eight (8) units. Namely: Adult Literacy 12 Months Barbing 6 Months Catering and Hotel Management 9 Months Computer Techniques and Operations 6 Months Hairdressing and Cosmetology 9 Months Photography 6 Months Sewing and Fashion 12 Months Textile Design 6 Months
